Suggested order

Numbers on the cards above follow this path — frequency of real-world use, not book order. Checkboxes track your progress (stored in this browser only).

The everyday eight. These cover most real-world code. Learn these first.
Strategy, Observer, Factory Method, Singleton, Builder, Adapter, Decorator, Facade

The workhorses. Powerful but situational. Learn once the first eight feel natural.
Command, Template Method, Composite, State, Proxy, Chain of Responsibility

Recognition level. You mostly need to spot these in other people's code.
Abstract Factory, Prototype, Bridge, Flyweight, Mediator, Memento, Iterator, Visitor, Interpreter
